Shipping Information

  • Shipping Locations: We currently deliver across India.
  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.
  • Delivery Time: Typically takes 3–7 business days depending on location.
  • Shipping Charges:
    - Free shipping on orders above ₹999.
    - ₹49 flat charge for orders below ₹999.
  • Courier Partners: Delhivery, DTDC, India Post.
  • Order Tracking: You will receive a tracking link via SMS/Email after dispatch.
  • Delays: In case of external delays (weather, strikes, etc.), we will notify you promptly.
  • Local Delivery (Phaltan): Same-day delivery available for orders before 2 PM.
